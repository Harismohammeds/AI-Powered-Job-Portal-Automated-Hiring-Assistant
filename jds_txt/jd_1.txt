Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)
Job Summary
A CRC manages day-to-day clinical trial activities at the site level, ensuring patient safety,
protocol adherence, and accurate data collection.
Key Responsibilities
• Recruit and screen study participants
• Obtain informed consent
• Coordinate patient visits and trial procedures
• Maintain study records and case report forms (CRFs)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
Qualifications
• BSc Nursing / Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Experience in hospital or research setting preferred
Skills
• Patient interaction and communication
• Clinical knowledge
• Data recording and reporting
Work Environment
• Hospitals, research sites, academic institutions
Career Path
CRC → CRA → Clinical Trial Manager
