Traveling CRA
Job Summary
A Traveling CRA is a highly mobile monitoring professional responsible for managing
multiple sites across regions or countries.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct frequent on-site monitoring visits across multiple locations
• Ensure consistency in trial execution across sites
• Handle site escalations and complex issues
• Coordinate with global teams
• Maintain high-quality monitoring standards
Qualifications
• Experienced CRA (usually CRA II/III level)
Skills
• Adaptability and flexibility
• Cross-cultural communication
• Advanced monitoring expertise
Work Environment
• Extensive domestic/international travel
Career Path
Traveling CRA → Global CRA → Clinical Trial Lead
