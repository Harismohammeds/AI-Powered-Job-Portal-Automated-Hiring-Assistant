Remote CRA (Virtual Monitoring)
Job Summary
A Remote CRA conducts monitoring activities virtually using digital platforms, focusing on
centralized and risk-based monitoring.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform remote Source Data Review (SDR)
• Monitor trial data via electronic systems (EDC)
• Identify data trends and risks
• Communicate with sites through virtual platforms
• Support decentralized clinical trials
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
Skills
• Digital literacy (EDC, CTMS systems)
• Data analysis
• Risk-based monitoring
Work Environment
• Remote or hybrid
Career Path
Remote CRA → Centralized Monitoring Lead → Clinical Data/Risk Manager
