Site Monitor / Clinical Monitor
Job Summary
A Site Monitor (Clinical Monitor) ensures that clinical trial sites comply with protocols,
regulatory requirements, and ethical standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor site performance and compliance
• Conduct site visits and audits
• Ensure patient safety and rights
• Review informed consent documentation
• Report deviations and risks
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
Skills
• Auditing and compliance
• Attention to detail
• Clinical knowledge
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, hospitals
Career Path
Site Monitor → Senior CRA → Clinical Trial Manager
