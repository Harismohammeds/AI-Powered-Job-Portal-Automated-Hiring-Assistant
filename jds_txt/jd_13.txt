Senior Clinical Research Associate
(Senior CRA)
Job Summary
A Senior CRA independently manages complex clinical trials, oversees multiple sites, and
ensures compliance with protocols and regulations while mentoring junior CRAs.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct and lead monitoring visits (SIV, MV, COV)
• Oversee multiple clinical trial sites
• Perform advanced Source Data Verification (SDV) and risk assessment
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Identify and resolve protocol deviations and site issues
• Mentor CRA I/II and junior staff
• Prepare high-level monitoring and audit reports
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• 3–6+ years of CRA experience
Skills
• Advanced monitoring expertise
• Leadership and mentoring
• Risk management
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Senior CRA → Lead CRA → Clinical Trial Manager
