Lead Clinical Research Associate (Lead
CRA)
Job Summary
A Lead CRA supervises a team of CRAs, ensuring consistent monitoring quality across sites
and acting as the primary liaison between the project team and monitoring staff.
Key Responsibilities
• Supervise and guide CRA teams
• Assign sites and monitoring responsibilities
• Ensure standardization of monitoring practices
• Review monitoring reports for quality and accuracy
• Act as escalation point for site issues
• Coordinate with Clinical Trial Managers and sponsors
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy degree
• 5–8+ years in clinical research
Skills
• Team leadership
• Project coordination
• Decision-making
Work Environment
• CROs, global pharma companies
Career Path
Lead CRA → Clinical Trial Manager → Project Manager
