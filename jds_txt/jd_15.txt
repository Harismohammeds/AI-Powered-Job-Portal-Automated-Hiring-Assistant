Principal CRA
Job Summary
A Principal CRA is a highly experienced expert responsible for handling high-risk, complex,
or global clinical trials and providing strategic monitoring oversight.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead monitoring strategy for complex trials
• Oversee global/multi-country sites
• Provide expert guidance on compliance and risk mitigation
• Support audits and regulatory inspections
• Train and mentor senior CRAs and lead CRAs
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams
Qualifications
• Advanced degree preferred (MSc, PharmD, etc.)
• 8–10+ years of CRA experience
Skills
• Strategic thinking
• Advanced regulatory knowledge
• Leadership and influence
Work Environment
• Large CROs, multinational pharma companies
Career Path
Principal CRA → Clinical Operations Director
