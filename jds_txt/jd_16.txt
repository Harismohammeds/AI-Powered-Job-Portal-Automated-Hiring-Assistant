Clinical Trial Lead (CRA Background)
Job Summary
A Clinical Trial Lead (CTL) oversees the end-to-end execution of clinical trials, ensuring
timelines, quality, and budgets are met.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage overall clinical trial operations
• Develop monitoring and execution strategies
• Coordinate cross-functional teams (data, regulatory, safety)
• Track timelines, budgets, and deliverables
• Ensure compliance with regulatory authorities like U.S. Food and Drug
Administration
• Handle risk management and escalation
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• 6–10+ years of clinical research experience
Skills
• Project management
• Leadership and communication
• Budget and timeline management
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Clinical Trial Lead → Clinical Project Manager → Program Director
