Monitoring Team Lead
Job Summary
A Monitoring Team Lead manages a team of CRAs and ensures high-quality monitoring
activities across all assigned clinical trials.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and manage CRA teams
• Monitor performance metrics and KPIs
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Conduct training and performance evaluations
• Review monitoring plans and reports
• Ensure inspection readiness
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• 6–9+ years of experience
Skills
• Team management
• Quality assurance
• Conflict resolution
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Monitoring Team Lead → Clinical Operations Manager → Director
