Oncology Clinical Research Associate
(Oncology CRA)
Job Summary
An Oncology CRA monitors clinical trials focused on cancer treatments, including
chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and targeted therapies, ensuring patient safety and protocol
compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor oncology trial sites (solid tumors, hematology studies)
• Verify complex clinical data (tumor response, imaging, biomarkers)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Review adverse events (serious and non-serious)
• Ensure proper handling of investigational oncology drugs
• Support investigators in protocol adherence
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Oncology experience or certification preferred
Skills
• Knowledge of oncology protocols and endpoints
• Understanding of adverse event grading (CTCAE)
• Analytical and clinical interpretation skills
Work Environment
• Cancer research centers, CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Oncology CRA → Senior Oncology CRA → Oncology Trial Lead
