Cardiology Clinical Research Associate
(Cardiology CRA)
Job Summary
A Cardiology CRA monitors trials related to cardiovascular diseases, including drug and
device studies.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor trials involving heart diseases (e.g., hypertension, heart failure)
• Review ECGs, imaging data, and cardiovascular endpoints
• Ensure compliance with trial protocols and safety standards
• Verify device/drug usage accuracy
• Coordinate with cardiologists and site staff
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Knowledge of cardiology preferred
Skills
• Understanding of cardiovascular diagnostics (ECG, echocardiography)
• Data interpretation skills
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Hospitals, cardiac research centers, CROs
Career Path
Cardiology CRA → Senior CRA → Clinical Trial Manager
