Junior Clinical Research Associate
Job Summary
A Junior CRA assists senior CRAs in monitoring clinical trials and ensures that studies
comply with protocols and regulatory requirements.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in site monitoring visits (on-site/remote)
• Verify data accuracy (Source Data Verification - SDV)
• Support site initiation, monitoring, and close-out visits
• Ensure regulatory compliance
• Prepare monitoring reports
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Clinical research training
Skills
• Analytical thinking
• Attention to detail
• Understanding of clinical trial processes
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Junior CRA → CRA I → CRA II → Senior CRA
