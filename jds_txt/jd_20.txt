Neurology Clinical Research Associate
(Neurology CRA)
Job Summary
A Neurology CRA manages trials focused on neurological disorders such as Alzheimer’s,
Parkinson’s, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor neurological clinical trials
• Evaluate neurological assessment scales and endpoints
• Ensure accurate data collection for cognitive and behavioral studies
• Monitor patient safety and adverse events
• Ensure protocol adherence
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Knowledge of neurology preferred
Skills
• Understanding of neurological assessments (MMSE, etc.)
• Analytical thinking
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Neurology research centers, CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Neurology CRA → Senior CRA → Specialized Trial Lead
