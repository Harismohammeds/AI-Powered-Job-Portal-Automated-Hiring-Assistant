Infectious Disease Clinical Research
Associate
Job Summary
An Infectious Disease CRA monitors trials related to infections such as HIV, tuberculosis,
influenza, and emerging diseases like COVID-19.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor infectious disease clinical trials
• Ensure biosafety and infection control protocols
• Verify laboratory data (viral load, cultures, PCR results)
• Track adverse events and disease progression
• Ensure regulatory compliance
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Microbiology / Pharmacy
Skills
• Knowledge of microbiology and infectious diseases
• Understanding of lab diagnostics
• Risk assessment
Work Environment
• Research labs, hospitals, CROs
Career Path
Infectious Disease CRA → Global Health CRA → Program Manager
