Vaccine Clinical Research Associate
(Vaccine CRA)
Job Summary
A Vaccine CRA monitors clinical trials for vaccine development, ensuring safety, efficacy,
and regulatory compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor vaccine trials (Phase I–IV)
• Track immunogenicity and safety data
• Ensure cold chain management and vaccine storage compliance
• Verify adverse event reporting (AE/SAE)
• Support large-scale immunization studies
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Biotechnology
Skills
• Knowledge of immunology and vaccinology
• Understanding of cold chain logistics
• Data monitoring and safety reporting
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, vaccine research institutes, global health organizations
Career Path
Vaccine CRA → Senior CRA → Vaccine Program Lead
