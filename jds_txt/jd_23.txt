Risk-Based Monitoring CRA (RBM
CRA)
Job Summary
A Risk-Based Monitoring CRA focuses on centralized and risk-driven monitoring approaches
to identify, assess, and mitigate risks in clinical trials.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform centralized monitoring using statistical and data-driven tools
• Identify high-risk sites, patients, and data points
• Reduce reliance on 100% Source Data Verification (SDV)
• Monitor Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Collaborate with data management and biostatistics teams
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Biostatistics / Pharmacy
• Knowledge of risk-based monitoring tools
Skills
• Data analytics and interpretation
• Risk assessment
• Use of EDC/CTMS systems
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies (remote/hybrid)
Career Path
RBM CRA → Centralized Monitoring Lead → Risk Manager
