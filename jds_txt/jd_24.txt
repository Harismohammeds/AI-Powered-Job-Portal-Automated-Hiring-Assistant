Regulatory Clinical Research Associate
(Regulatory CRA)
Job Summary
A Regulatory CRA ensures that all clinical trial activities comply with regulatory
requirements across different countries.
Key Responsibilities
• Prepare and submit regulatory documents to authorities
• Ensure compliance with agencies like U.S. Food and Drug Administration and
European Medicines Agency
• Manage ethics committee and institutional review board submissions
• Maintain regulatory documentation and approvals
• Track regulatory changes and implement updates
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s in Pharmacy / Life Sciences
• Regulatory affairs certification preferred
Skills
• Knowledge of global regulatory frameworks
• Documentation and compliance
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Regulatory CRA → Regulatory Specialist → Regulatory Affairs Manager
