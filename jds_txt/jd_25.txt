Quality Assurance CRA (GCP Auditor)
Job Summary
A Quality Assurance CRA (GCP Auditor) ensures clinical trials adhere to quality standards
and regulatory guidelines through audits and inspections.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct internal and external GCP audits
• Evaluate trial processes, systems, and documentation
• Identify compliance gaps and recommend corrective actions
• Prepare audit reports and inspection readiness plans
• Ensure adherence to Good Clinical Practice
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Experience in clinical research and auditing
Skills
• Auditing and inspection skills
• Analytical thinking
• Knowledge of quality systems
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, regulatory bodies
Career Path
QA CRA → Quality Manager → Head of Quality Assurance
