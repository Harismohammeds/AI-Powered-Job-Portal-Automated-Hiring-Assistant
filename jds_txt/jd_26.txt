Pharmacovigilance CRA
Job Summary
A Pharmacovigilance CRA focuses on monitoring drug safety within clinical trials and post-
marketing phases.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor adverse events (AEs) and serious adverse events (SAEs)
• Perform safety data review and signal detection
• Ensure timely safety reporting to authorities
• Maintain pharmacovigilance databases
• Ensure compliance with global safety regulations
Qualifications
• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences
Skills
• Drug safety knowledge
• Medical terminology
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
PV CRA → Drug Safety Scientist → PV Manager
