Medical Device CRA
Job Summary
A Medical Device CRA monitors clinical trials involving medical devices such as implants,
diagnostic tools, and surgical equipment.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor device clinical trials and ensure proper device usage
• Verify device accountability and tracking
• Ensure compliance with device-specific regulations
• Train site staff on device handling and protocols
• Review safety and performance data
Qualifications
• Degree in Biomedical Engineering / Life Sciences / Pharmacy
Skills
• Knowledge of medical devices and regulations
• Technical understanding of device functionality
• Monitoring and compliance
Work Environment
• Medical device companies, CROs, hospitals
Career Path
Medical Device CRA → Device Trial Manager → Regulatory Specialist
