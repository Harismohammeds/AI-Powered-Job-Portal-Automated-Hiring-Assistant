Bioequivalence (BE) Study Monitor
Job Summary
A Bioequivalence Study Monitor oversees studies comparing generic drugs with reference
drugs to ensure equivalent bioavailability.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor bioequivalence studies (PK/PD studies)
• Verify sample collection, storage, and analysis procedures
• Ensure compliance with regulatory guidelines
• Review pharmacokinetic (PK) data and reports
• Ensure study protocol adherence
Qualifications
• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences
Skills
• Knowledge of pharmacokinetics (PK) and pharmacodynamics (PD)
• Analytical and data review skills
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs, bioequivalence centers
Career Path
BE Monitor → Clinical Pharmacology Specialist → Trial Manager
