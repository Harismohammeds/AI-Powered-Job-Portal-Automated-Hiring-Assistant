Site Clinical Research Associate (Site
CRA)
Job Summary
A Site CRA works directly at the clinical trial site (hospital/research center), ensuring that
studies are conducted according to protocol, regulatory standards, and sponsor requirements.
Key Responsibilities
• Coordinate daily clinical trial activities at the site
• Ensure protocol adherence and patient safety
• Maintain site documentation (ISF – Investigator Site File)
• Support monitoring visits and audits
• Assist investigators in trial execution
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
Qualifications
• BSc Nursing / Life Sciences / Pharmacy
Skills
• Clinical operations knowledge
• Coordination and communication
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Hospitals, clinical research sites
Career Path
Site CRA → CRA → Senior CRA
