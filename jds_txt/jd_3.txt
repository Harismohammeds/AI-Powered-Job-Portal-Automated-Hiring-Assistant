Site Management Associate (SMA)
🧾 Job Summary
An SMA supports clinical trial site management activities, acting as a bridge between the
sponsor/CRO and investigator sites.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in site selection and feasibility assessment
• Support site onboarding and training
• Maintain site communication and documentation
• Track site performance and compliance
• Assist CRAs with monitoring activities
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Clinical research certification
Skills
• Coordination and communication
• Project tracking
• Organizational skills
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
SMA → CRA → Lead CRA
