Hospital Research Coordinator
Job Summary
A Hospital Research Coordinator manages clinical trials within a hospital, acting as the link
between patients, investigators, and sponsors.
Key Responsibilities
• Recruit and screen patients for clinical trials
• Obtain informed consent from participants
• Schedule and coordinate patient visits
• Collect and manage clinical data
• Ensure patient safety and ethical compliance
• Maintain study records and documentation
Qualifications
• Nursing / Life Sciences / Pharmacy background
Skills
• Patient interaction and counseling
• Clinical knowledge
• Data management
Work Environment
• Hospitals, academic medical centers
Career Path
Research Coordinator → CRA → Clinical Trial Manager
