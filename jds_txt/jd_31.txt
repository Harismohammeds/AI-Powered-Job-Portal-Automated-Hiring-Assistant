Investigator Site Manager
Job Summary
An Investigator Site Manager (ISM) oversees all operational aspects of clinical trials at a
specific site, ensuring efficient study execution and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage site staff and research teams
• Oversee multiple clinical trials simultaneously
• Ensure compliance with protocols and regulations
• Coordinate with sponsors, CROs, and monitors
• Manage site budgets and resources
• Ensure audit and inspection readiness
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Experience in clinical research/site coordination
Skills
• Leadership and team management
• Project coordination
• Problem-solving
Work Environment
• Hospitals, research institutes
Career Path
Site Manager → Regional Site Manager → Clinical Operations Manager
