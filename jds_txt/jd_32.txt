Clinical Trial Site Manager
Job Summary
A Clinical Trial Site Manager is responsible for the overall management and performance of
clinical trials at a site, ensuring timelines, quality, and compliance are met.
Key Responsibilities
• Oversee end-to-end clinical trial operations at the site
• Manage site staff, investigators, and coordinators
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Monitor site performance metrics (recruitment, data quality, timelines)
• Coordinate with sponsors, CROs, and regulatory bodies
• Handle audits and inspections
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• 3–7+ years of clinical research experience
Skills
• Leadership and management
• Clinical operations expertise
• Communication and reporting
Work Environment
• Hospitals, multi-specialty research centers
Career Path
Site Manager → Clinical Trial Manager → Operations Head
