CRA – CRO-Based
Job Summary
A CRO-based CRA works for a Contract Research Organization, managing clinical trials on
behalf of multiple sponsors (pharmaceutical/biotech companies).
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor clinical trial sites across different sponsor projects
• Conduct Site Initiation, Monitoring, and Close-Out Visits
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Manage multiple protocols and therapeutic areas
• Prepare monitoring reports and documentation
• Coordinate with sponsors and site staff
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Clinical research certification preferred
Skills
• Multi-project management
• Adaptability
• Strong communication
Work Environment
• CROs (fast-paced, multi-client environment)
Career Path
CRA → Senior CRA → Lead CRA → Project Manager
