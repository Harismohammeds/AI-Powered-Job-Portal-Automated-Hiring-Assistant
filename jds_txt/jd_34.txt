Sponsor-Dedicated CRA
Job Summary
A Sponsor-Dedicated CRA works exclusively for one sponsor (pharma/biotech company)
while being employed by a CRO or directly by the sponsor.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor trials for a single sponsor
• Ensure consistency across all assigned studies
• Develop deep understanding of sponsor protocols
• Build long-term relationships with sites and stakeholders
• Maintain high-quality monitoring standards
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• CRA experience required
Skills
• Sponsor-specific expertise
• Relationship management
• Consistency and quality focus
Work Environment
• CROs or pharma companies
Career Path
Sponsor CRA → Senior CRA → Clinical Trial Lead
