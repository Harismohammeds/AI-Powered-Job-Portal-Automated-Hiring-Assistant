Project CRA
Job Summary
A Project CRA plays a hybrid role combining monitoring responsibilities with project
coordination and oversight.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage site monitoring activities within a project
• Support Clinical Trial Managers in project execution
• Track timelines, deliverables, and site performance
• Coordinate communication between teams
• Assist in risk management and issue resolution
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy degree
• Experience as CRA II/III
Skills
• Project coordination
• Communication and reporting
• Problem-solving
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Project CRA → Clinical Trial Manager → Project Manager
