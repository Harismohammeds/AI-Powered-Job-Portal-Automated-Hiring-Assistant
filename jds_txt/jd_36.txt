Global Clinical Research Associate
Job Summary
A Global CRA manages and monitors clinical trials across multiple countries, ensuring
compliance with international regulations and global study standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor international clinical trial sites
• Ensure compliance with global regulatory authorities
• Coordinate with cross-country teams
• Manage cultural and operational differences
• Support global trial execution strategies
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Experience in international trials preferred
Skills
• Cross-cultural communication
• Knowledge of global regulations
• Adaptability
Work Environment
• Global CROs, multinational pharma companies
Career Path
Global CRA → Global Trial Lead → Program Manager
