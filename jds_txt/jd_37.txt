Regional Clinical Research Associate
Job Summary
A Regional CRA oversees clinical trial monitoring within a specific geographic region (e.g.,
South India, APAC, Europe).
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor sites within assigned region
• Ensure regional regulatory compliance
• Coordinate with local investigators and teams
• Track regional performance metrics
• Manage travel and site visits efficiently
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
Skills
• Regional regulatory knowledge
• Time and territory management
• Communication
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Regional CRA → Global CRA → Clinical Operations Manager
