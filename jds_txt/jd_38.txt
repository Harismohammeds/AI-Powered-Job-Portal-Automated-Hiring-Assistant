Clinical Trial Manager (CTM)
Job Summary
A Clinical Trial Manager (CTM) oversees the execution of one or more clinical trials,
ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and in compliance with regulations.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage end-to-end clinical trial operations
• Develop study timelines, budgets, and monitoring plans
• Supervise CRAs and site teams
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Track trial progress, risks, and deliverables
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams (data, regulatory, safety)
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• 5–8+ years of clinical research experience
Skills
• Project management
• Leadership and decision-making
• Budget and timeline management
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
CTM → Clinical Project Manager → Program Manager
