Clinical Project Manager
Job Summary
A Clinical Project Manager oversees multiple clinical trials or large-scale projects, ensuring
strategic alignment and successful delivery.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage multiple clinical trials simultaneously
• Develop project strategies and execution plans
• Oversee budgets, timelines, and resource allocation
• Lead cross-functional teams
• Ensure compliance with global regulations
• Communicate with sponsors and stakeholders
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy degree
• 7–10+ years of experience
Skills
• Strategic planning
• Leadership and communication
• Risk and resource management
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Project Manager → Program Manager → Director
