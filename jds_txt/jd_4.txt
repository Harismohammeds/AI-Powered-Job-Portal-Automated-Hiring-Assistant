Clinical Data Associate
Job Summary
A Clinical Data Associate ensures accurate collection, validation, and management of clinical
trial data.
Key Responsibilities
• Enter and validate clinical trial data
• Perform data cleaning and discrepancy management
• Review Case Report Forms (CRFs)
• Work with data management systems (EDC)
• Ensure data integrity and compliance
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Biostatistics / Pharmacy
Skills
• Data analysis
• Attention to detail
• Knowledge of clinical databases
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, data centers
Career Path
Data Associate → Clinical Data Manager → Biostatistician
