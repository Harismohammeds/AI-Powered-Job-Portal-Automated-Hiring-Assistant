Program Manager (Clinical Research)
Job Summary
A Program Manager oversees a portfolio of clinical projects, ensuring alignment with
organizational goals and long-term strategy.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage multiple clinical programs or therapeutic areas
• Align projects with business and regulatory strategies
• Oversee multiple project managers and teams
• Track overall program performance and KPIs
• Manage budgets and high-level risks
• Interface with senior stakeholders
Qualifications
• Advanced degree preferred (MBA / MSc / PharmD)
• 10+ years of clinical research experience
Skills
• Strategic leadership
• Portfolio management
• Decision-making
Work Environment
• Large pharma companies, global CROs
Career Path
Program Manager → Director → VP
