Director of Clinical Operations
Job Summary
The Director of Clinical Operations leads the planning, execution, and oversight of all
clinical trials within an organization or division.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement clinical operations strategies
• Oversee multiple programs and trial portfolios
• Ensure compliance with regulatory bodies like U.S. Food and Drug Administration
• Manage large teams (CRAs, CTMs, Project Managers)
• Optimize processes and operational efficiency
• Oversee budgets, resources, and timelines
• Support inspections and audits
Qualifications
• Advanced degree (MBA / MSc / PharmD preferred)
• 12–15+ years of experience
Skills
• Executive leadership
• Strategic planning
• Organizational management
Work Environment
• Multinational pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Director → Senior Director → VP Clinical Operations
