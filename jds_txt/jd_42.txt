Head of Clinical Research
Job Summary
The Head of Clinical Research is a top executive responsible for the overall clinical research
strategy, innovation, and execution within an organization.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and lead global clinical research strategy
• Oversee all clinical development programs
• Ensure regulatory compliance across regions
• Drive innovation in clinical trials (AI, decentralized trials)
• Manage senior leadership teams
• Represent the organization to regulators and stakeholders
• Align clinical research with business goals
Qualifications
• Advanced degree (PhD / MD / PharmD / MBA)
• 15–20+ years of experience
Skills
• Visionary leadership
• Strategic decision-making
• Stakeholder management
Work Environment
• Global pharma companies, biotech firms
Career Path
Head → VP → Chief Medical Officer (CMO)
