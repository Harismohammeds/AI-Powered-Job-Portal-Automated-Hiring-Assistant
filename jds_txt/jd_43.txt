Decentralized Trial CRA (DCT CRA)
Job Summary
A Decentralized Trial CRA manages clinical trials conducted using virtual, hybrid, or home-
based models, reducing the need for physical site visits.
Key Responsibilities
• Oversee decentralized and hybrid clinical trials
• Monitor patient data collected via wearable devices and apps
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice in virtual settings
• Coordinate telemedicine visits and remote patient interactions
• Ensure data privacy and digital consent processes
• Support logistics like home sample collection
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Knowledge of decentralized trial models
Skills
• Digital health technologies
• Remote monitoring tools
• Data privacy awareness
Work Environment
• Remote / hybrid (CROs, digital health companies)
Career Path
DCT CRA → Digital Trial Lead → Innovation Manager
