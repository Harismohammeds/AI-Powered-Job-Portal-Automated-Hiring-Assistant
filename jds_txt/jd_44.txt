eClinical Systems Specialist
Job Summary
An eClinical Systems Specialist manages and supports digital platforms used in clinical trials,
ensuring smooth data flow and system functionality.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage systems like EDC (Electronic Data Capture), CTMS, eTMF
• Configure and maintain clinical trial software
• Train staff on system usage
• Ensure data integrity and system compliance
• Troubleshoot technical issues
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / IT / Health Informatics
Skills
• Knowledge of clinical software systems
• Technical troubleshooting
• Data management
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, health tech firms
Career Path
eClinical Specialist → Systems Manager → Digital Clinical Lead
