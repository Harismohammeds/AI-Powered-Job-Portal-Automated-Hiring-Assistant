Remote Monitoring CRA
Job Summary
A Remote Monitoring CRA performs centralized monitoring activities using digital tools,
minimizing the need for on-site visits.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform remote Source Data Review (SDR)
• Monitor trial data through EDC systems
• Identify trends, risks, and anomalies
• Communicate with sites virtually
• Support risk-based monitoring strategies
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy
Skills
• Data analysis
• Digital tools (EDC, CTMS)
• Risk assessment
Work Environment
• Fully remote or hybrid
Career Path
Remote CRA → RBM Specialist → Central Monitoring Lead
