Clinical Data Reviewer
Job Summary
A Clinical Data Reviewer analyzes clinical trial data to ensure accuracy, consistency, and
compliance with protocols.
Key Responsibilities
• Review clinical data for discrepancies and errors
• Validate data against source documents
• Perform data cleaning and query management
• Collaborate with data management and biostatistics teams
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Biostatistics / Pharmacy
Skills
• Data analysis and interpretation
• Attention to detail
• Knowledge of clinical databases
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, remote setups
Career Path
Data Reviewer → Clinical Data Manager → Biostatistician
