AI-Driven Trial Monitoring Specialist
Job Summary
An AI-Driven Trial Monitoring Specialist uses artificial intelligence and advanced analytics
to optimize clinical trial monitoring and decision-making.
Key Responsibilities
• Implement AI tools for trial monitoring and risk prediction
• Analyze large datasets for patterns and anomalies
• Automate monitoring workflows
• Support predictive analytics for patient recruitment and retention
• Collaborate with data science and clinical teams
Qualifications
• Life Sciences + Data Science / AI background
Skills
• AI/ML tools and analytics
• Data modeling
• Programming (Python/R preferred)
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs, AI healthcare startups
Career Path
AI Specialist → Clinical Data Scientist → Digital Health Leader
