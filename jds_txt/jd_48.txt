Regulatory Affairs Specialist (Clinical
Research)
Job Summary
A Regulatory Affairs Specialist ensures that clinical trials meet all regulatory requirements
for approval, conduct, and reporting across different regions.
Key Responsibilities
• Prepare and submit regulatory documents (IND, NDA, CTA, etc.)
• Manage submissions to regulatory authorities
• Ensure compliance with local and global regulations
• Track regulatory approvals and timelines
• Interpret regulatory guidelines and implement changes
• Support clinical teams in maintaining compliance
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Pharmacy / Life Sciences
Skills
• Regulatory knowledge (global frameworks)
• Documentation and submission expertise
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs, regulatory consulting firms
Career Path
Regulatory Specialist → Regulatory Manager → Director of Regulatory Affairs
