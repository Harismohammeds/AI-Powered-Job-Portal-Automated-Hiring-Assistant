GCP Compliance Auditor
Job Summary
A GCP Compliance Auditor ensures that clinical trials adhere to Good Clinical Practice and
regulatory standards through audits and inspections.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct internal and external audits of clinical trials
• Evaluate compliance of sites, processes, and documentation
• Identify gaps and recommend corrective/preventive actions (CAPA)
• Prepare audit reports and support inspections
• Ensure inspection readiness
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Experience in clinical research and quality assurance
Skills
• Auditing and inspection skills
• Analytical thinking
• Knowledge of regulatory guidelines
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, regulatory agencies
Career Path
Auditor → Senior Auditor → Quality Director
