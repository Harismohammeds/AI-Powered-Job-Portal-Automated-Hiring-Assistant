Regulatory Affairs Assistant
Job Summary
Supports regulatory processes to ensure clinical trials comply with national and international
regulations.
Key Responsibilities
• Prepare and submit regulatory documents
• Maintain regulatory files and approvals
• Track submissions to authorities like U.S. Food and Drug Administration
• Assist in ethics committee submissions
• Ensure compliance with regulatory standards
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s in Pharmacy / Life Sciences
Skills
• Knowledge of regulatory guidelines
• Documentation skills
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs, regulatory agencies
Career Path
Regulatory Assistant → Regulatory Affairs Specialist → Manager
