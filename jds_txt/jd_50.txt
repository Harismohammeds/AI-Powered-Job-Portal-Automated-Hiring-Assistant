Clinical Quality Manager
Job Summary
A Clinical Quality Manager oversees quality systems in clinical research to ensure
compliance, efficiency, and continuous improvement.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement quality management systems (QMS)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Manage audits, inspections, and CAPA processes
• Monitor quality metrics and KPIs
• Train staff on quality standards and procedures
• Drive continuous improvement initiatives
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Experience in QA/QC roles
Skills
• Quality management systems
• Leadership and training
• Risk management
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Quality Manager → Senior Manager → Director of Quality
