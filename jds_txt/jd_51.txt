Ethics Committee Coordinator
Job Summary
An Ethics Committee Coordinator manages the review and approval process of clinical trials
to ensure ethical standards and patient rights are protected.
Key Responsibilities
• Coordinate Institutional Ethics Committee (IEC) / IRB meetings
• Review clinical trial protocols and informed consent documents
• Ensure ethical compliance and patient safety
• Maintain ethics committee documentation and records
• Communicate decisions to investigators and sponsors
• Track approvals, amendments, and renewals
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing / Law
Skills
• Knowledge of research ethics and regulations
• Documentation and coordination
• Communication skills
Work Environment
• Hospitals, research institutions, regulatory bodies
Career Path
Ethics Coordinator → Ethics Committee Manager → Regulatory Head
