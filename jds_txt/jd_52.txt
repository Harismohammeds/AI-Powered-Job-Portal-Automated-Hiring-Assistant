Clinical Quality Assurance Auditor
Job Summary
A Clinical Quality Assurance (QA) Auditor evaluates clinical trial processes, systems, and
documentation to ensure compliance with regulatory standards and internal quality policies.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct internal and external audits of clinical trials and vendors
• Review Trial Master Files (TMF), protocols, and study reports
• Identify compliance gaps and deviations
• Recommend Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA)
• Prepare detailed audit reports
• Ensure adherence to Good Clinical Practice
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Experience in clinical research or QA
Skills
• Auditing and inspection
• Analytical thinking
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
QA Auditor → Senior Auditor → Quality Director
