GCP Auditor
Job Summary
A GCP Auditor specializes in auditing clinical trials specifically against Good Clinical
Practice standards to ensure ethical and scientific integrity.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct site, system, and process audits
• Ensure compliance with GCP and regulatory guidelines
• Audit investigator sites and clinical data
• Identify risks and non-compliance issues
• Support regulatory inspections
• Develop audit plans and schedules
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Experience in clinical monitoring or QA
Skills
• Deep knowledge of GCP
• Risk identification
• Documentation review
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies, regulatory bodies
Career Path
GCP Auditor → Lead Auditor → QA Manager
