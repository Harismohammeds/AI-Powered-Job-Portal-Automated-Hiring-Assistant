Inspection Readiness Specialist
Job Summary
An Inspection Readiness Specialist ensures that clinical trials and organizations are fully
prepared for regulatory inspections by authorities such as U.S. Food and Drug Administration
and European Medicines Agency.
Key Responsibilities
• Prepare clinical sites and teams for regulatory inspections
• Conduct mock audits and gap assessments
• Ensure documentation completeness and accuracy
• Coordinate responses to regulatory queries
• Train staff on inspection protocols
• Maintain inspection readiness plans
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Experience in QA or regulatory roles
Skills
• Audit preparation
• Regulatory knowledge
• Communication and training
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Inspection Specialist → QA Manager → Compliance Director
