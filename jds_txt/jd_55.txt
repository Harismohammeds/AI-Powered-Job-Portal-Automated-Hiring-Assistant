Clinical Compliance Manager
Job Summary
A Clinical Compliance Manager ensures that all clinical trial activities comply with
regulatory requirements, internal policies, and industry standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement compliance programs
• Monitor adherence to Good Clinical Practice
• Oversee audits, inspections, and CAPA processes
• Identify compliance risks and implement mitigation strategies
• Ensure alignment with global regulations
• Train teams on compliance standards
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• 6–10+ years of clinical research experience
Skills
• Risk management
• Leadership and decision-making
• Regulatory expertise
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
Compliance Manager → Senior Manager → Director of Compliance
