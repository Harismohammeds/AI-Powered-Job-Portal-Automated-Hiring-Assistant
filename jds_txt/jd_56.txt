CRA Trainer
Job Summary
A CRA Trainer is responsible for training aspiring and junior Clinical Research Associates in
clinical trial processes, monitoring techniques, and regulatory compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and deliver training programs for CRAs and clinical research staff
• Teach monitoring processes (SIV, MV, COV, SDV, SDR)
• Provide training on Good Clinical Practice and regulatory requirements
• Conduct workshops, webinars, and practical sessions
• Assess trainee performance and provide feedback
• Develop training materials and SOP-based modules
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Strong CRA experience (typically 4–8+ years)
Skills
• Teaching and presentation skills
• Communication and mentoring
• Clinical research expertise
Work Environment
• Training institutes, CROs, freelance platforms
Career Path
CRA Trainer → Senior Trainer → Training Director
