Clinical Research Consultant
Job Summary
A Clinical Research Consultant provides expert advice to organizations on clinical trial
design, execution, compliance, and optimization.
Key Responsibilities
• Advise on clinical trial strategies and protocols
• Provide guidance on regulatory compliance
• Optimize trial processes and efficiency
• Support audit readiness and inspections
• Assist in vendor selection and site management
• Provide expertise across therapeutic areas
Qualifications
• Advanced degree (MSc / PharmD / PhD preferred)
• 8–15+ years of experience
Skills
• Strategic thinking
• Problem-solving
• Industry expertise
Work Environment
• Independent consulting, CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
Consultant → Senior Consultant → Independent Advisor
