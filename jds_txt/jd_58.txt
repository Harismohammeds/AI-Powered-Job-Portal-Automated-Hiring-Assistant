Freelance CRA
Job Summary
A Freelance CRA works independently on contract-based assignments, providing monitoring
and clinical trial support services to sponsors and CROs.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct monitoring visits (on-site or remote)
• Perform Source Data Verification (SDV)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Prepare monitoring reports
• Manage site communication and issue resolution
• Support short-term or project-based trials
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Prior CRA experience (usually 3–5+ years)
Skills
• Self-management
• Flexibility and adaptability
• Strong monitoring expertise
Work Environment
• Remote, contract-based, project-specific
Career Path
Freelance CRA → Independent Consultant → Clinical Advisor
