Clinical Trial Auditor (Independent)
Job Summary
An Independent Clinical Trial Auditor conducts unbiased audits of clinical trials, ensuring
compliance with regulatory and quality standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform independent audits of clinical trial sites and systems
• Evaluate compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Identify gaps and recommend CAPA
• Prepare audit reports for sponsors and regulatory bodies
• Support organizations during inspections
• Provide third-party quality assurance services
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy
• Strong QA/auditing experience
Skills
• Auditing and compliance
• Analytical thinking
• Attention to detail
Work Environment
• Independent consulting, contract-based
Career Path
Auditor → Lead Auditor → Independent QA Consultant
