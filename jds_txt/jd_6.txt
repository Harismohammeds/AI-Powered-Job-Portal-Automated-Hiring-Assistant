Pharmacovigilance Associate
Job Summary
A Pharmacovigilance Associate monitors drug safety by collecting, assessing, and reporting
adverse drug reactions.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze adverse event reports
• Perform case processing and safety assessments
• Submit safety reports to authorities
• Maintain drug safety databases
• Ensure compliance with global safety regulations
Qualifications
• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences
Skills
• Analytical and medical knowledge
• Attention to detail
• Understanding of drug safety regulations
Work Environment
• Pharma companies, CROs
Career Path
PV Associate → Drug Safety Scientist → PV Manager
