Research Methodology Trainer
Job Summary
A Research Methodology Trainer educates students and professionals on clinical research
design, biostatistics, and scientific methodologies.
Key Responsibilities
• Teach research design and clinical trial methodology
• Train on study protocols, data analysis, and reporting
• Conduct workshops on biostatistics and evidence-based research
• Guide students in thesis and research projects
• Develop educational content and training modules
Qualifications
• MSc / PhD in Life Sciences / Biostatistics / Clinical Research
Skills
• Teaching and academic expertise
• Statistical knowledge
• Research design skills
Work Environment
• Universities, training institutes, online platforms
Career Path
Trainer → Senior Trainer → Academic/Research Director
