Clinical Research Associate (CRA I, II,
III)
Job Summary
CRAs are responsible for monitoring clinical trials to ensure they are conducted according to
protocol, regulatory requirements, and ethical standards. Levels (I, II, III) indicate increasing
experience and responsibility.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct Site Initiation Visits (SIV), Monitoring Visits (MV), and Close-Out Visits
(COV)
• Perform Source Data Verification (SDV)
• Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice
• Review Case Report Forms (CRFs) and trial data
• Identify protocol deviations and implement corrective actions
• Train site staff on protocols and procedures
• Prepare detailed monitoring reports
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing
• Clinical research certification preferred
Skills
• Monitoring & auditing skills
• Regulatory knowledge
• Communication and reporting
Level Differences
• CRA I: Entry-level monitoring, supervised
• CRA II: Independent monitoring, manages multiple sites
• CRA III: Advanced monitoring, handles complex/global trials
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies
Career Path
CRA I → CRA II → CRA III → Senior CRA
