In-House Clinical Research Associate
Job Summary
An In-House CRA supports trial monitoring activities remotely from the office, focusing on
documentation, data review, and site communication.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform remote site monitoring and data review
• Track study progress and timelines
• Review regulatory documents and trial master files
• Communicate with sites for issue resolution
• Support field CRAs with administrative tasks
Qualifications
• Degree in Life Sciences / Pharmacy
Skills
• Strong documentation and coordination
• Remote monitoring tools proficiency
• Analytical skills
Work Environment
• CROs, pharma companies (office-based/remote)
Career Path
In-House CRA → Field CRA → Senior CRA
