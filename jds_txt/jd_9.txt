Field Clinical Research Associate
Job Summary
A Field CRA conducts on-site monitoring visits at clinical trial sites, ensuring protocol
compliance and data accuracy.
Key Responsibilities
• Travel to investigator sites for monitoring visits
• Verify source data and patient records
• Ensure site compliance with protocols and regulations
• Train site staff and resolve operational issues
• Manage site relationships
Qualifications
• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Nursing background
Skills
• Strong interpersonal skills
• Problem-solving
• Time and travel management
Work Environment
• Field-based role with frequent travel
Career Path
Field CRA → Senior CRA → Lead CRA
